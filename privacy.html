Privacy Policy

How we collect, use, and protect your information

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

Motiv Medical ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our medical equipment services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect personal information that you provide to us or that is provided by your healthcare provider, including:

  • Contact information (name, address, phone number, email address)
  • Medical information related to prescribed equipment
  • Insurance information and coverage details
  • Prescription and physician order information

Usage Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device and usage patterns, including IP addresses, browser types, and pages visited.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To provide medical equipment services as prescribed by your healthcare provider
  • To coordinate equipment delivery, setup, and maintenance
  • To process insurance claims and handle billing matters
  • To communicate with you regarding your equipment and services
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements
  • To improve our services and website functionality

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Healthcare Providers: With your referring physicians and healthcare team for care coordination
  • Insurance Companies: For claims processing and coverage verification
  • Service Providers: With third-party vendors who assist with equipment delivery and maintenance
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law, regulation, or court order
  • Business Operations: With our affiliates and subsidiaries for business purposes

SMS Opt-In Data: Mobile information will not be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. All the above categories exclude text messaging originator opt-in data and consent; this information will not be shared with any third parties.

Important: We maintain strict confidentiality agreements with all service providers and ensure they adhere to the same privacy standards we maintain.

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:
